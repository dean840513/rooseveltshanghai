THE ROOSEVELT HOUSE

Where a Name Becomes Heritage.

A historic house, restored for today.

The House of Roosevelt is set within a neoclassical landmark first built in 1920.

In 2008, the Roosevelt family redeveloped the building, preserving its character and restoring its former grandeur for a new era.

Here, the Roosevelt name, Shanghai’s historic architecture,
and the culture of a private membership club come together.

Its appeal is not defined by the view alone.
The depth of a members’ club, the refinement of wine and dining, the poise of a historic building,
and the stories of those who gather here are what make Roosevelt distinct.

Not every room is open to everyone.
That is part of what makes a night at Roosevelt so intriguing.

Yet through the rooftop, dining, wine and private occasions,
guests visiting Shanghai can experience a glimpse of the Roosevelt atmosphere.

Not fully open. Never ordinary.Roosevelt is not simply a place to look out over Shanghai at night.
It is a way of stepping into it.
